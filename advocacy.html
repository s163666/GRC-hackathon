#5: "Over two-thirds of low-income women do not struggle to cover the cost of menstrual products. (F)

According to an article by Reuters, two-thirds [of surveyed low-income women in the US] couldn't afford menstrual hygiene products such as tampons or pads during the previous year.

Period poverty is a huge issue facing millions globally. In fact, over 500 million people lack access to menstrual products and hygiene facilities. In order to help more people gain access to quality menstrual products and end period poverty, here are some organizations to support:
