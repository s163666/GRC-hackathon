Menstrual Maze is a game committed to destigmatizing periods and raising awareness about period poverty. A major problem people face globally a lack of access to period products. Period products are expensive, and millions of young people are left struggling without them.
In addition, the taboo that surrounds periods often hinders critical discussion about issues such as period poverty. With Menstrual Maze, we hope to show players that it's okay to discuss periods and to provide resources for further education and advocacy.
